# F3 Nation Settings: Home Screen

This screen is the central hub for F3 Nation app settings.

## What you see
Buttons (may vary by role/permissions):

- **Calendar Settings**
- **Region Info**
- **User Settings**
- **Emergency Info Access**
- **Help Menu**
- **Welcomebot Settings**
- **Reporting Settings**
- **SLT Settings**
- **Backblast & Preblast Settings** (label may be truncated on screen)
- **Backblast Email Settings**
- **Custom Field Settings**
- **Paxminer Mapping**

## Common tasks
### Open the Help Menu
- Click **Help Menu**  
Use this when you want the quick “how-to” guides (calendar, preblast, etc.).  
See: `f3nation-help-menu-calendar-preblast.md`

### Update your profile & region
- Click **User Settings**  
See: `f3nation-screen-user-settings.md`

### Emergency info lookup (use only for genuine emergencies)
- Click **Emergency Info Access**  
See: `f3nation-screen-emergency-info-access.md`

## Notes
- Some buttons may be visible only to admins/region leadership.
- Use the **Close** button to exit the settings modal.
