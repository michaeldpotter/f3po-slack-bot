# User Settings Screen

Use this screen to configure your F3 Nation profile and emergency contact details.

> Navigation: Settings Home → **User Settings** (see `f3nation-screen-settings-home.md`)

---

## Top section

### Username
A text field labeled **Username**.

Note shown on screen: this username is used to identify you globally.  
Tip: follow the on-screen guidance (e.g., don’t include your home region in the username).

### Home Region
A dropdown labeled **Home Region** (example shown: Wheaton).

### Start Date Override (optional)
A date picker labeled **Start Date Override (optional)**  
Use only if you need to override your official start date.

### My Stats
Button labeled **My Stats** (with a link icon).

---

## Profile picture

### New Profile Picture (optional)
- Click **Upload File** to set a Nation dashboard avatar.
- Note shown: this avatar can be different from your Slack avatar.

---

## Emergency Contact Information

### Enable Downrange Access? (optional)
Checkbox labeled **Enable**.

Meaning (from on-screen text):
- If enabled, users from other regions who have opted into downrange sharing can search for your info.

### Emergency Contact (optional)
Text field.

### Emergency Contact Phone (optional)
Text field.

### Emergency Contact Notes (optional)
Text area for notes “in case of an emergency” (example guidance includes allergies/medical).

---

## Save changes
- Click **Submit** to save.
- Click **Close** to exit without saving.
