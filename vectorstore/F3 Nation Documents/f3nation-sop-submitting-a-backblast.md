# **📝 Submitting a Backblast**

**Purpose:**  
 This SOP outlines the process for submitting a backblast after leading a workout (Q) using the F3 Nation Slack-integrated system.

**Platform:**  
 F3 Nation Slack App

**Reference Video:**  
 [F3 Nation \- How to Submit a Backblast](https://youtu.be/tRAVOFbtHgY?si=3pVUShI1adC4_sIz)

---

## **🧭 Step-by-Step Instructions**

### **1\. Access the Backblast Submission**

You have two options:

* **Option A:** Click the notification from F3 Nation prompting you to submit a backblast.  
* **Option B:** Use the Slack search bar:  
  * Type **“back blast”**  
  * Click **“Create a Backblast”**

### **2\. Select the Past Q**

* A prompt will appear to choose a past Q.  
* Click the dropdown to view your previous Qs.  
* Select the workout you want to submit a backblast for.

### **3\. Add Attending PAX**

* A list of PAX will appear.  
* Select those who attended.  
* If someone is missing, manually add them.  
* Click **Done** when finished.

### **4\. Optional: Add “Downrange” PAX**

* In the Downrange PAX line you can search for PAX who attended a beatdown but are not in your Slack.  
* All PAX who are members of your Slack should be tagged in the normal PAX section regardless of home region.  
* You can also manually list untagged PAX using comma-separated names.

### **5\. Enter Total PAX Count**

* This may auto-populate based on selections.  
* You can manually enter the count if needed.

### **6\. Fill Out the Moleskin**

* Add a summary or reflection from the workout.  
* Example: Copy/paste notes from your Q.  
* Click **Done** after entering.

### **7\. Optional Features**

* **Email Backblast to PAX:** Enable this if you want it sent directly.  
* **Custom Fields:** These vary by region and admin setup.  
  * Example: “Fartsack” field to note who bailed last minute.  
* **Upload File:** Attach relevant media (e.g., “Boy Band” image or file).

### **8\. Final Review and Submit**

* Review all entries for accuracy.  
* Click **Submit**.

### **9\. Confirmation**

* You’ll receive a notification from F3 Nation confirming submission.  
* The backblast will appear in the AO channel as a posted file.

---

## **✅ Best Practices**

* Submit your backblast promptly after your Q.  
* Use the moleskin to reflect on the workout and share insights.  
* Tag all attending PAX accurately to maintain records.  
* Use custom fields creatively to enhance engagement.
