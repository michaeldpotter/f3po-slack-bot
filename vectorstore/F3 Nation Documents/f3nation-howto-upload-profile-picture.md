# How to Upload a Profile Picture (Nation Dashboard Avatar)

This sets your **Nation dashboard avatar**, which can be different from your Slack avatar.

## Steps
1. Open **F3 Nation Settings**.
2. Click **User Settings**.
3. Scroll to **New Profile Picture (optional)**.
4. Click **Upload File** and select an image.
5. Click **Submit**.

## Notes
- If you upload a new file but don’t click **Submit**, it may not save.
