# **📝 Signing Up to Q**

**Purpose:**  
 This SOP outlines the step-by-step process for signing up to lead a workout (Q-Spot) using the F3 Nation Slack-integrated calendar.

**Platform:**  
 F3 Nation Slack App

**Reference Video:**  
 [F3Nation \- How To Sign up for a Q-Spot](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4A1UroeVA0s&list=PLChC4Hw8Z8x2wG6yk1FwtTKeIySfHT9we)

---

## **🧭 Step-by-Step Instructions**

### **1\. Access the F3 Nation Calendar**

* Open Slack.  
* Use the search bar to type **“calendar”**.  
* Click on **“Open F3 Nation Calendar”** from the search results.

### **2\. View and Filter Calendar**

* Optional: View the calendar image to see upcoming Q-Spots for the next two weeks.  
* Optional: Filter by **AO (Area of Operation)** if you know where you want to Q.  
  * Example: Select “Center for Ants” and click **Done**.

### **3\. Show Available Q-Spots**

* Click **“Show only open Q slots”** to filter out taken spots.  
* Identify an available date (e.g., July 27).  
* Click **“More”** next to the date.  
* Click **“Take Q”** to claim the spot.

### **4\. Confirm Your Spot**

* After claiming, the slot will disappear from the open list.  
* To verify, click **“Show only my events”** to view your scheduled Q.

### **5\. Customize the Pre-Blast**

* Click into the event to edit the **Pre-Blast** message.  
  * Example: Update “What” to “Up, Beat, Down”.  
  * Example: Update “Why” to “To get 1% better”.  
* Note: Templates and tags (e.g., VQ, Maniversary) can be modified by admins.

### **6\. Schedule the Pre-Blast**

* Choose when to send the Pre-Blast:  
  * **Send now**  
  * **Send the day before the event** (recommended for future dates)  
* Click **Update** to finalize.

---

## **🛠 Admin Capabilities**

* Modify templates and event tags.  
* Add new tag options for special events.

---

## **✅ Best Practices**

* Always double-check your AO and date before confirming.  
* Use meaningful Pre-Blast messages to motivate participants.  
* Schedule reminders to avoid missing your Q.
